Part Collection



We wished to develop a system that allows the microalgae grow on the filter developed in the Wet Lab to grow and manage smoothly. The system replaces filters(micro algae grows on) at regular intervals, circulates wastewater, maintains proper pH and temperature, and controls LED lights for growth.
