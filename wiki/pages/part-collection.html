Part Collection




We wished to develop a system that allows the microalgae grow on the filter developed in the Wet Lab to grow and manage smoothly. The system replaces filters(micro algae grows on) at regular intervals, circulates wastewater, maintains proper pH and temperature, and controls LED lights for growth.



Structure

Filter replacement works in a similar way to a Ferris wheel. Several filters are hung from a rotating chain, and as the chain rotates, the filters are fed or discharged as wastewater. This structure is not only very easy to replace the filter, but also the replacement cycle of the filter can be easily changed. All filters can be replaced at once on the day when microalgae growth is completed, or it can be operated so that one sheet is inserted and discharged per day.




Control
All hardware is controlled by AVR Microprocessor. It is most suitable for operating our system because it is easy to control various hardware. Users manage the system through the web interface.
