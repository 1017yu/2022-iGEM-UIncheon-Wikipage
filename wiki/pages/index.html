Releasing purified water after purification involves the risk of leaking genetically modified organisms to external environment.
That problem can be resolved enough with existing treatment method, but microalgae are less sensitive than other microorganisms.
They don't be treated clearly in general treatment systems.
Thus, to ensure biosafety, we have to monitor the leak of genetically edited microalgae using a sensistive sensor.
An intrinsic biosensor of microalgae, that's what we're trying to make.
